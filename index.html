About Us

Pashusanjeevani Services (OPC) Pvt. Ltd. is a DPIIT-recognized startup and Udyam-registered micro-enterprise, delivering doorstep veterinary services across Karnataka. We use technology, telemedicine, and trained field staff to provide reliable animal healthcare solutions to livestock owners in both rural and urban areas.

Our Services

Telemedicine

Consult certified veterinarians via mobile from anywhere.

Field Veterinary Support

Trained Veterinary Field Executives deliver treatment, vaccination & AI at your farm.

Animal Health Training

Skill-based training for AHWs, AI Technicians, and para-vets with ASCI-aligned modules.
